About The Project

TAOT.CO is a creative studio focused on transforming simple tote bags into hand-painted wearable artwork. The project explores the relationship between everyday objects and artistic expression.

Instead of existing only inside galleries, art becomes something that can be carried and used every day. Each tote bag is treated as a canvas where illustration, colour and graphic composition come together.

The goal of the studio is to create objects that feel personal, creative and expressive while remaining functional for daily life.

The Founders

TAOT.CO was founded by Ahmed Haroon and Humna Affan. Both founders share a strong passion for illustration, design and visual storytelling.

Through this project they explore how art can move beyond traditional spaces and become part of everyday life through functional objects.

Each tote bag produced by the studio reflects experimentation with colour, graphic design and character-driven illustration.
