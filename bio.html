About The Project

TAOT.CO is a creative project focused on transforming simple tote bags into hand painted wearable artwork.

Each bag begins as a blank canvas. Through illustration, colour and composition it becomes a unique piece that blends art and functional design.

The studio focuses on small batch creations rather than mass production, ensuring that each tote bag carries an individual identity.

The Founders

TAOT.CO was founded by Ahmed Ali Haroon and Humna while studying fine arts.

Both founders share a passion for illustration, design and visual storytelling.

The studio allows them to experiment with new artistic ideas while creating functional objects that people can use every day.
