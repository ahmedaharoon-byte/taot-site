About TAOT

TAOT.CO is a creative studio founded by Ahmed and Humna. The platform focuses on visual culture, fashion direction, photography, filmmaking, and digital media.

Ahmed Ali Haroon is a multidisciplinary creative artist working across film, photography, graphic design, music production, and fashion. Humna contributes creative direction and collaborative development within the TAOT platform.

The goal of TAOT is to build a strong creative identity that merges fashion, culture, and storytelling through modern digital platforms.
