About TAOT

TAOT.CO is an independent creative studio focused on hand painted tote bags. Each bag is treated as a small artwork, combining illustration, design and personal expression rather than mass production.

The studio was created by Ahmed Ali Haroon and Humna while studying fine arts, with the goal of turning everyday accessories into wearable art pieces.

Founder

Ahmed Ali Haroon is a multidisciplinary artist working across filmmaking, design, photography and visual storytelling. His work moves between digital media and physical art objects.
