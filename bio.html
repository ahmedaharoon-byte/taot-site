About TAOT

TAOT.CO is a creative studio founded by Ahmed and Humna. Two fine arts students creating sustainable art through hand painted tote bags and custom artwork.

The studio focuses on transforming simple tote bags into unique illustrated pieces combining painting, design, and contemporary visual culture.

Founder

Ahmed Ali Haroon is a filmmaker, designer, model and multidisciplinary artist based in Islamabad. He works across filmmaking, graphic design, photography and visual storytelling.
