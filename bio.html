About TAOT

TAOT.CO is a creative studio founded by Ahmed and Humna. The brand focuses on visual storytelling, fashion, photography, design, and artistic collaboration.

Ahmed Ali Haroon is a multidisciplinary creative working across filmmaking, photography, design, and music. Humna collaborates in creative direction and artistic development of the brand.

The studio explores experimental visual culture and sustainable art practices including upcycled fashion and handmade creations.
