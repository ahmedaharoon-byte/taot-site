About The Project

TAOT.CO was created as a creative collaboration focused on turning tote bags into small pieces of visual art. Instead of treating bags as simple accessories the project explores them as portable canvases.

Every design is painted by hand and each bag becomes a one of a kind object. Illustration, graphic design and colour experimentation all play a role in shaping each piece.

The project also celebrates sustainability by transforming simple reusable tote bags into long lasting creative objects.

The Founders

TAOT.CO was founded by Ahmed Ali Haroon and Humna while studying fine arts. Both founders share a passion for illustration, design and visual storytelling.

Their goal was to create a small studio where art could exist outside traditional galleries and instead live within everyday life. A tote bag becomes something personal, expressive and creative rather than simply practical.

The studio continues to experiment with new visual styles and artistic directions while staying rooted in handmade craftsmanship.
