About

TAOT.CO (The Art Of Tote) is a creative studio founded by Ahmed and Humna. We are two Fine Arts students with a passion for sustainable art and handcrafted design.

Our work focuses on transforming everyday items into artistic pieces. We upcycle tote bags into unique hand-painted creations that combine functionality with artistic expression.

Each piece is individually designed and painted, making every tote bag a one-of-a-kind artwork. Our goal is to promote creativity, sustainability, and personal expression through wearable art.
