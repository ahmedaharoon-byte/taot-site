About TAOT

TAOT.CO is a creative studio founded by Ahmed Ali Haroon and Humna. The brand focuses on transforming simple tote bags into hand painted pieces of wearable art. Each tote is painted manually, making every piece unique rather than mass produced.

Combining illustration, design and contemporary visual culture, the studio treats each tote bag as a small artwork that people can carry and use in everyday life.
