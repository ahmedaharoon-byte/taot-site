The Brand

TAOT.CO is an independent creative studio focused on handcrafted tote bags. The project explores the idea that everyday objects can become expressive art pieces. Each tote is painted manually which means no two bags are identical.

Instead of mass produced accessories the studio treats every bag as a canvas. Illustration, typography, colour and composition are combined to create pieces that are both functional and artistic.

The goal of the brand is simple. Transform ordinary materials into objects that carry personality, creativity and storytelling.
