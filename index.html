The Brand

TAOT.CO is an independent creative studio focused on transforming simple tote bags into artistic objects. Each tote bag becomes a canvas where illustration, graphic design and colour experimentation come together.

Rather than mass production every tote is hand painted, which ensures each piece carries its own personality. The studio treats everyday objects as opportunities for creative expression.

The project explores how art can exist within daily life. A tote bag becomes more than an accessory. It becomes a portable artwork.
